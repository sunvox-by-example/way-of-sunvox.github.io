About

Way of SunVox is a resource for learning SunVox and an aggregator of MetaModules, projects, and techniques.

It is run by Mark Reveley (aka mrev on forum) and is open for contributions by the community. The github repo can be found here, pull requests are welcome. If you are not familiar with github, you can submit content/corrections/suggestions to me via email at mreveley (at) gmail.com.
