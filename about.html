About

Way of SunVox is a resource for learning SunVox and an aggregator of MetaModules, projects, and techniques.

It was created by yours truly (Mark Reveley aka mrev on forum) though is open for contributions by the community. The github repo can be found here, pull requests are welcome. If you are not familiar with github, you can submit content/corrections/suggestions to me via email at mreveley (at) gmail.com.

You can hear some of my music made in SunVox on my Soundcloud

I also play and produce in the band Dirtwire
